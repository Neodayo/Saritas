Our Story

Sarita Macansantos Midel

She completed her education in Zamboanga, where she also took on sewing sideline jobs while studying. Later on, she moved to Manila and studied for two years to become a couturier.

She started her business in Manila in 1993, then later moved it to Zamboanga City. They spent five years at the Shopping Center, primarily sewing uniforms, but they also made gowns, barongs, and did clothing repairs. After that, they moved to Panigayan, behind the Water District, where they stayed for about 17 years. At that time, the business was not yet called "Sarita's"—it was known as Grooms and Bride.

In 2012, they started offering gown rentals and custom-made clothing, eventually stopping uniform sewing. That's when the business officially became known as Sarita's Wedding Creation.
